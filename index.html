Gravity Orbit Applet

Planet masses: 1.00 M⊕ each
1. Choose Place / Move Star, then drag the orange star.
2. Choose Place / Move Planet 1 or Planet 2, drag that planet somewhere, then click-drag from it to set its launch velocity.
3. Distances use AU by default. Toggle to km for engineering notation. Choose Drag View to pan around the space. Right-click drag, middle-click drag, or hold Space also pans. Mouse wheel zooms. Use Gravity: Star Only / Full Gravity to toggle planet–planet interactions.
